Who We Are

STREAMING

SanecaTV Radiomeltdown is an independent broadcast network operating out of the G4E Movement Studio in Lithonia, Georgia, under the Saneca TV LLC / XOCO Broadcast Network umbrella. Established in 2021, STV has been a platform for indie artists, underground talent, mainstream acts, and AI music — culture-driven and community-built from day one.

We are part of the SENSTACK media brand — a creative ecosystem bringing highlighting broadcast production. STV is the broadcast arm, and radiomeltdown.com is the home base.

Our mission is simple: give independent artists a platform, give the community a voice, and keep the frequency live — always.
