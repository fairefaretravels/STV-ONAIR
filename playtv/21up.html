🏆 Companion Game

TIMEOUT RULES

A drinking game for the Timeout Sports Talk trivia deck — built for the homegirls, played over FaceTime.
🔞 21+ only. Drink water between rounds, know your limits, and never play if anyone's driving after.
01Miss a Question
SIP
🟢 Easy question, wrong answer — everyone clowns you for free.
2 DRINKS
🟡 Medium question, wrong answer.
3 DRINKS
🔴 Hard question, wrong answer — and you're benched, skip your next turn.
02Format Twists
DRINK
📋 Multiple choice — guess wrong, you drink. Guess right but admit you had no clue? Everyone else drinks for the "lucky guess."
GROUP SIP
🔥 Debate question — anyone who disagrees with the group consensus drinks. Voices get raised? Everybody drinks together.
HOST DRINKS
🎙 Open answer — if NOBODY on the call gets it, whoever's hosting that round drinks for stumping the squad.
03Bonus Chaos
SIP
"Wait, I knew that" energy after hearing the answer — drink.
SIP
Caught Googling mid-question — no cheating, drink.
PASS IT
A bonus fact drops a number, year, or record and you call it within 5 seconds — make someone else drink instead.
04Team Mode (Optional)
DRINK
Split into two teams using the app's Score tab. Losing team drinks every time the other team scores. First to 10 points calls the next category.
DARE
Halfway through (Q25), whoever's behind on the Score tab gets to hand the leader one drink-or-dare.
🍻 Live Drink Tally
Keep it honest during the call — tap + every time someone drinks.
