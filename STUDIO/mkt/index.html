📞 Cold Call Opener

Stage 1 · Cold Callers
Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] with [Company] — do you have 60 seconds? I'm reaching out because [reason/trigger — e.g. "we work with businesses like yours on X"]. Quick question — are you currently dealing with [pain point the service solves]?

✅ If They Answer & Engage

Stage 2 · Follow-Up Team
Got it, that makes sense — a lot of the folks we work with run into that too. Would it be alright if I send over some info on how we help with that? Takes 2 minutes to look at, no obligation. [If hesitant] No worries — mind if I follow up in a week once you've had a chance to think it over?

💬 No-Answer Text

Non-Answer Follow-Up
Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from [Company] — tried reaching you today about [reason]. No pressure, just let me know if you'd like more info or a better time to chat!

📧 No-Answer Email

Non-Answer Follow-Up
Subject: Quick question about [pain point] Hi [Name], I tried calling earlier — wanted to reach out about [reason/trigger]. If this is relevant to you, happy to send over more info or hop on a quick call whenever works. Let me know!
