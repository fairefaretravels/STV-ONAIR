The Artist

Emil D. Gordon, Sr.

Emil D. Gordon, Sr. is a Detroit-rooted artist whose music moves between R&B warmth, soul depth, and hip-hop swagger. Raised on the sounds of a city that built the beat of America, Emil brings an authentic voice that blends old-school sensibility with contemporary edge.

His Hear My Music EP established him as a storyteller with range — and now Cruzin' captures that Detroit spirit in full color: lowriders, city lights, and two people making a memory on Woodward Ave.
